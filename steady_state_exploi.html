Steady-state exploration of Spicknall`s models

The purpose of this part is to explore the steady states of models mentionned in Spicknall`s review with \(\epsilon\) varying from 0 to 0.9. For each model, the parameters and the initial conditions are the same, where applicable. Every model starts with S=0.98, Iw=0.01 and Iz=0.01 at t=0. We used th same parameters as in the basic models: \(\beta\) w = 0.04, \(\beta\) z = 0.015, \(\gamma\) = 0.01, \(\gamma\)`= 0.1, \(\rho\) = 0.5 and \(\phi\)= 0.05.
